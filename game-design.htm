Tetris

My attempt at creating Tetris true to the aesthetic and gameplay of the classic GameBoy version. It's written in Java and, hey, if you want, take a look at the code.

Psychosomatic RPG

How does the psychological state in your party affect their ability in combat? How does the death of a beloved teammate wrack the rest of your party? These are the sort of questions that I want players to engage with. It's definitely taking inspiration from Darkest Dungeon, but with more of a focus on camaraderie and deeper psychology than the brutal stress system of Darkest Dungeon.

Spacer

This is going to be a game about space combat. The general idea is that you're the captain of a starship -- so you see everything the starship captain does. You have access to all the sensor data and ship inventory and you have to figure out where the enemies lie and how to best dispatch them with the resources you have in deep space -- including all the celestial bodies around you. Maybe you'll slingshot around a moon and engage in close combat! Maybe you'll hide in a nebula until the enemy reveals themself! The only rule here is an adherence to the laws of hard sci-fi.
